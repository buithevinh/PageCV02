about

Tieu Long Nu's name (literally "Little Dragon Maiden") is based on the year she was born, the Year of the Dragon according to the Chinese zodiac. Her real name is unknown and is never mentioned in the novel; she is referred to as "Tieu Long Nu" by other people, and as "Long'er" by Yang Guo, her teacher and Granny Sun.
